What is
Decentralized Administration Voting (DAV)

The best way to bring to society what they want is to let them raise the issue and vote for the solution. It's the basic principle which we use in our model.
For example, you want to sell your old smartphone and there are some of the parameters which already exist, but you don't have such a parameter as battery capacity. And that's where you start your DAV carrier by adding a new parameter for the community voting.
Your issue goes to a pool of voters and after voting battery capacity parameter is added. Both parties gain USE token in certain proportions.
Such mechanism lies in all types of voting activities, such as: adding branches to catalog, adding parameters (numeric/symbolic, numeric/range), dublicating, merging, items' branch changing, censorship surveilliance and etc.

What is
API+PSR

API - Application Programming Interface - allows us to interact with different platforms (for example, Facebook's Graph API) for collecting permitted users' data for the PSR purposes.

PSR - Permanet Social Research - is a mechanism of structuring collected personal data with subsequent implementation of cohort analysis, generating respondents to clusters and defining clusters' hierarchy.

API + PSR = new age of e-commerce marketing which relies on the most native priciples of human society. Mission of such method is not the attempt to meet the demand (sometimes, an out of date demand), like it's ussually done with regular e-commerce marketing, but generating the demand with custom product feed using the individuals' cluster distribution. As a result sellers don't need to spend their time and money on marketing and can focuse on the quality of ther offers and development of Usagle ecosystem.

  1. Molotok.ru close down

    August 20th, 2015

    The biggest and the most convenient Russian marketplace Molotok.ru, the analog of Ebay, had been ruined and closed. Since 1999 it's gained its' popularity as the best Internet-auction and covered more than 60% of customer-to-customer e-commerce sales with the total turnover of 77 million dollars (1,1% of whole Russian e-commerse market). However, nothing can protect even the greatest projects from poor management and administration. In 2012 new owners came, and it was the beginning of the end, starting with additional fees for all users - ending with poor administration in all aspects.

  2. The idea of the project

    Q4 2016

    Idea of the project of New-Age Marketplace emerged in consequence of absence of considerable substitute for Russian users. Until nowadays the problem hasn't been solved for users who have to post a solo product in a number of primitively classified advertisements, websites and thematic forums, even trying to use foreing solutions for internal sales. Russian e-commerce market is a good start for such kind of project as ours due to the fact that there is still a huge demand in it. With a good start we have a great chance to push aside international e-commerce giants.

It took us weeks analizing all the available resourses regarding existing problems and inconveniences of modern marketplaces. After that we spent a bunch of time developing a smart solutions for each aspect of our New-Age Marketplace.
